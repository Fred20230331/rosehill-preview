International Student Story
From Day One,
She Belonged.
Theana Chen · International Student Leader 2024

Meet Theana Chen. 🇳🇿

She arrived at Rosehill from China three years ago. On her very first day, a classmate walked over and said hello. She says that's the moment she decided to stay.

Three years later, she's Rosehill's International Student Leader for 2024 — now doing for new arrivals exactly what was done for her.

This is what school culture actually looks like. Not a values statement on a wall. A cycle of welcome that keeps going, one student at a time.

Thinking about New Zealand secondary education for your child? Our International Student Office answers questions in Chinese or English.
