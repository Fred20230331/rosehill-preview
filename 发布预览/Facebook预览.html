She left Rosehill in 2012. On April 29, she fights for a WBC World Title. 🥊 Mariah Turner — known in the ring as "The Golden Girl" — is one of ours. A Rosehill College alumna from South Auckland who turned a local passion into a professional career that has taken her around the world. Her record speaks for itself: 12 wins, 1 loss, 6 knockouts, and now the IBF International Featherweight Title around her waist. Next stop: Melbourne, for a shot at the WBC Interim World Super Bantamweight Title against Skye Nicolson — broadcast live on DAZN and Fox Sports Australia. We couldn't be prouder. Mariah's journey is built on the same values we instil in every student: Hihiritanga — the willingness to keep striving, even when it's hard. Go get it, Mariah. 🇳🇿 #RosehillCollege #MariahTurner #TheGoldenGirl #WBCTitle #ProBoxing #SouthAuckland #Hihiritanga
