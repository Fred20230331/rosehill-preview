Not all classrooms have four walls. 🎭 At Rosehill College, some of the most powerful learning happens on stage — through Drama, Dance, Music, and our award-winning Kapa Haka programme. Kapa Haka isn't just performance. It's language, history, identity, and belonging — all woven together. Our group has competed at Polyfest and brought home recognition that the whole school celebrates. For students who thrive in the creative arts, Rosehill offers a full pathway from Year 9 through to NCEA Level 3. ✔ Drama & Theatre Productions ✔ Classical and Contemporary Music ✔ Dance (including Pacific styles) ✔ Kapa Haka — Māori cultural performance ✔ Visual Arts This is education that stays with you. #RosehillCollege #PerformingArts #KapaHaka #NewZealandSchool #AucklandArts #NZEducation #MāoriCulture #Polyfest
